    About Art History Project

    The project that our teacher assigned to us was an Art History Project on any influential people who had an impact on Art throughout the years and then try to capture their "essence" in a form of our own art. Seeing as I wanted to try to capture the "American Dream" I choose a person who influenced many artists to capture that dream "Norman Rockwell".

    About Human Rights Poster

    This is another project that my teacher assigned us this one was a poster. This assignment we had to choose a human rights article and try to base our poster around it. I choose Article 3, which states that. “Everyone has the right to life, liberty and security of person”. The real reason why I choose to do my poster on Gun Violence is becasue of the epidemic of Gun Violence that has been sweaping the nation as of late.
